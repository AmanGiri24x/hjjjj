                              QUICK HELP
================================================================================

Q: Why can't I push?
A: You have a 147MB file in git history (GitHub limit is 100MB)

Q: Will I lose my code?
A: No! The script backs up everything to .git-backup

Q: What about node_modules?
A: They won't be pushed. Your friend will run "npm install" to get them

Q: How long does it take?
A: About 1-2 minutes to run the script and push
